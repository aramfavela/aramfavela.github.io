What we can do:

Consumer Protection (Federal Laws) Federal laws protect people from the predatory and wrongful practices of debt collectors, telemarketers, credit reporting agencies, and identity theft. We help protect people under three primary areas of federal legislation: (a) the Telephone Consumer Protection Act ("TCPA"), (b) the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act ("FDCPA"), and the Fair Credit Reporting Act ("FCRA").

Consumer Debt Defense Unfortunately, over the past few years, our nation's financial crisis has led many people to default on their financial obligations. The debt collection industry has boomed, and as an unintended result, so have violations of the law that govern how those debts are collected. We represent debtors who are being sued or been sued. We do not provide debt settlement or debt consolidation services. We handle cases in the courts of law of California and Nevada.
