Pricing to Sell

As it sits, the market has extremely low inventory creating a high demand for good properties making it a seller’s market. What this means for sellers is there is very steep competition for buyers who are forced to make quick decisions. Over the past year, many sellers have found themselves with multiple offer situations. Let us help you decide which ones to counter and give advice on which may be the best offer to accept.

Although it is a seller’s market it is key to price your home correctly. The last thing you want is to overprice your home and have it on the market for an extended period of time and have it earn the stigma of something being wrong with it. Four Brothers Real Estate can provide you with comparative market analysis to help determine the fair market value of your home.

Another vital phase to selling a home is to properly market it. While it may be enough to put a “for sale” sign we can assist by creating brochures, fliers, postcards, and use other marketing tools such as online home-buying search engines. A majority of buyers use the internet to search for homes and it will only increase over time with millennium buyers slowly integrating themselves into the market. We can also help by utilizing the multiple listing service and taking advantage of other online tools to help put your home in front of the largest number of buyers and their agents as possible.

Other opportunities to take advantage of selling your home quickly are services like OBEO or home staging companies. These companies help your home stand out and often result in a quicker sale and higher offer. Before you sell your home it’s always good to take an impartial look at what you are offering inside and out.
