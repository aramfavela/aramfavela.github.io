Is it the Right Time?

As I am sure you already know they process to buying a home can be very complex. There are a handful of different parties, deadlines, contingencies, conditions, and planning that going into a decision like this so it is very important that you understand each step and are well prepared.

Both new and experienced buyers have their own set of financial considerations that they must look into before committing to a home. New buyers should take a look at their budget to determine if they can afford a monthly mortgage payment. The move-up buyer should look at their financial situation and determine that they are in fact ready to take the burden of a more expensive monthly payment. All buyers should be prepared to cover the down payment, closing cost, and any other cost that may arise. We are here to help you with these sorts of things and discuss the different option you may have which in some cases can lower or eliminate many cost. Perhaps even more important is to be sure that you meet the basic criteria a lender may ask of any applicant which includes a stable job, good credit among other items.
